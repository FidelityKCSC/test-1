Execution Process

01. Mandate & Requirement Intake

We receive formal mandates, Letters of Intent (LOI), or defined requirements from buyers and partners, including product specifications, target pricing, quantities, delivery terms, and overall commercial framework.

02. Offer Sourcing & Market Alignment

We engage with verified suppliers and counterparties, review Soft Corporate Offers (SCO) or Full Corporate Offers (FCO), and align both sides on pricing, specifications, and transaction feasibility.

03. Counterparty Verification & Due Diligence

We conduct structured due diligence on all participants, verifying corporate credentials, operational capability, and transaction readiness to ensure credible and qualified engagement.

04. Deal Structuring & Negotiation

We coordinate negotiations between buyer and seller, refine commercial terms, and align key elements including pricing structure, delivery conditions, and contractual responsibilities.

05. Contract Finalization & Financial Instruments

We support the drafting and review of contracts and financial instruments, including Letters of Credit (LC), SBLC, or other banking mechanisms, ensuring clarity, compliance, and bankability prior to execution.

06. Transaction Coordination & Execution

We oversee the transaction process, coordinate documentation flow, and maintain structured communication between all parties to ensure disciplined and efficient execution.

07. Closing & Completion

We follow through to final delivery, payment confirmation, and successful transaction closure, ensuring all contractual obligations are fulfilled.
